Matrix Engineers Progress
 Matrix New World Engineering is a company of engineering and science experts who focus on some of the nation’s most pressing long-term challenges, including climate change, water supply, disaster response and urban revitalization. Applying our expertise to areas where these challenges are becoming particularly acute, Matrix intends to further focus our offerings and expertise on these and other emerging National Strategic Issues, where solving environmental challenges through careful planning and engineering is becoming an indispensable measure of success.

National Reach
Matrix is a growing woman-owned business with offices in New York, New Jersey, Arizona, Texas, and Louisiana, with project experience throughout the U.S. and Caribbean.

Mission
 To contribute to the success of our clients by providing an unmatched combination of our team's technical excellence, practical experience, and the highest level of quality professional engineering services possible. Matrix is committed to creating a healthy work environment with the opportunity for all our staff to grow professionally.

To continually achieve this mission, Matrix strives to identify areas of self-improvement, including improving financial performance and profitability of our projects, without sacrificing the quality of the professional services offered to our clients.

Vision
 To be recognized as the premier woman-owned engineering firm in the United States, by offering superior, creative technical engineering expertise, relentlessly pursuing improvement in the quality of our work; hiring only people who can demonstrate the potential to grow; constantly seeking to transfer our values and knowledge internally; knowing our clients and their needs better than any other firm in our field.
